Before you reach out

Frequently asked

Quick answers to the most common questions we get.

🆓 Is there really no cost?

Absolutely none. Every prosthetic hand we provide is completely free to the recipient, no exceptions.

📦 How long does it take to get a hand?

Typically 2–4 weeks from first contact to delivery, depending on your location and customization needs.

🌍 I'm outside the US — can you help?

Yes! We work with partner organizations in 12+ countries. Reach out and we'll connect you with your local partner.

Not sure where to start? 🤝

Just send us a message — we'll figure out the best way to help together.
