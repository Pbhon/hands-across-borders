Who We Are

Our Mission

Every person deserves the ability to reach, hold, create, and connect — regardless of where they were born or what they were born with.

Mission Statement
"Our goal is to provide accessible, affordable, and custom prosthetics to combat the increasing costs of healthcare while allowing for individuals with limb loss or limb difference to participate in society without hindrances."
Our Story

Started with a single print.

Founded in January 2026, we’ve assembled a team of highschoolers that believes everyone deserves the ability to participate in society without hindrances. To combat the increasing costs of healthcare, our aim is to provide a cheaper, still effective, solution to as many of the 5.6+ million people currently living in the United States with limb loss or limb difference.

At the moment, we are focused on those with limb differences below the wrist. To aid us in this venture, please contact us at handsacrossborder@gmail.com as we are working to gain clients who are willing to provide feedback for our hands. We will adapt models to specific requirements given by clients.
