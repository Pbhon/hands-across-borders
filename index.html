How It Works

From filament to freedom
One print at a time

Every hand starts in our lab and ends in the hands of someone whose life it changes.

🖨️

3D Print

Industrial-grade 3D printers produce every component with precision and care — engineered for durability and comfort.

🔧

Assemble

Our skilled volunteers carefully assemble and test every hand, custom fitted to meet each individual's unique needs.

📦

Distribute

We deliver across Ohio and partner globally to reach those who need them most — at absolutely no cost to recipients.

❤️

Follow Up

We stay in touch, checking in with every recipient to ensure their hand fits well and offering adjustments as needed.

Our Process

From design to delivery

1

Design & Customize

We work with recipients to choose the right design for their lifestyle.

2

Print Components

Our printers run around the clock producing high-quality parts.

3

Quality Check

Every part is inspected for strength, fit, and finish before assembly.

4

Assembly

Volunteers join, fasten, and calibrate each hand with care.

5

Deliver with Love

We personally deliver or ship each hand and celebrate every moment.
