Who We Are

Our Mission

Every person deserves the ability to reach, hold, create, and connect — regardless of where they were born or what they were born with.

Mission Statement
"Hands Across Borders exists to empower individuals with below-elbow limb differences by providing freely crafted, 3D-printed prosthetic hands — built with precision in Ohio, delivered with love across the world. We believe access to assistive technology is a human right, not a privilege."
Our Story

Started with a single print.

Hands Across Borders began when our founder printed their first prosthetic hand for a neighbor's child in Columbus, Ohio. Witnessing the immediate impact made it clear — this had to be bigger.

We serve children and adults with below-elbow limb differences, partnering with hospitals, clinics, schools, and nonprofits to reach individuals who would otherwise go without.

The Technology

What goes into every hand.

🔩

Medical-Grade PLA

Biocompatible, food-safe filaments that are durable, lightweight, and safe for skin contact.

🖨️

FDM 3D Printing

Fused deposition modeling gives precise, repeatable results on every component.

📐

Open-Source Designs

We use and contribute to open-source designs from e-NABLE and humanitarian engineering communities.

🎯

Custom Fitting

Each hand is individually sized and adjusted directly with the recipient for comfort and function.
